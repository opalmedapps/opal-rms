Button and Symbol Legend

Button/Symbol Description
Call Patient Indicates the patient has not been called to a room. Click to call the patient to a room.
Call Patient Indicates that the patient has been called to a room. Click on the button to refresh the patient name on the tv screens.
Undo Call Click to return the patient to the waiting room.
Enter Weight Click to update the patient's weight.
Enter Weight Indicates that the patient has been weighed within the last 3 weeks. Click to update the patient's weight.
Enter Weight Indicates that the patient has been weighed today. Click to update the patient's weight.
Send For Weight Click to signal that the patient needs to be weighed.
Assign Exam Room Click to assign a patient to an exam room.
Assign Exam Room Indicates that the patient is already in an exam room. Click to move the patient to another exam room.
View Questionnaires Click to view the patient's questionnaires.
View Questionnaires Indicates that the questionnaire has been reviewed within the week that the patient has completed it. Click to view the patient's questionnaires.
View Questionnaires Indicates that the patient completed a questionnaire that has not been reviewed. Click to view the patient's questionnaires.
Complete Appointment Click to check a patient out of the Virtual Waiting Room.
Indicates that the patient has text message alerts turned on.
Indicates that the patient is on the Opal phone app.
